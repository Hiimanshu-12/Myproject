Famous Players

Indian cricket has produced some of the finest players:

Rohit Sharma

Known for his record-breaking double centuries in ODIs, he is one of the top modern batsmen.

Virat Kohli

One of the most consistent modern-day batsmen and a fitness icon.

Suryakumar Yadav

A dazzling T20 batsman known for his unorthodox shots and explosive batting.

Hardik Pandya

An aggressive all-rounder, Hardik is known for his power-hitting and seam bowling.

Jasprit Bumrah

Regarded as one of the best fast bowlers in modern cricket, especially in death overs.
